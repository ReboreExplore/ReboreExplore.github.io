**Ingredients** : Small pieces of boneless chicken, salt, turmeric, chilly poweder, pepper, corn flour, gram flour(besan), tomato ketchup, refined or sunflower oil, coriander leaves and onion (optional).

**Preparation Time** : 15 mins

-  Cut the chicken into very small pieces and wash it thoroughly.
- Add some salt, turmeric, chilly powder, pepper and mix them with the cut out chicken pieces.
- Mix well and store in tn the fridge for 3-4 hours.
- Put the chicken masala mix out of the fridge and put some cornflour and "besan" in 2:1 proportion. Add one small teaspoonful of tomato ketchup and mix everything.
- The chicken pieces should have a layer a cornflour and besan coating after the mix.
- Take a frying pan and pour in some refined oil. 
- After the pan is hot put the chicken pieces one after the other till it gets a golden brown color.
- Put the chicken out of the pan in a kitchen tissue paper so that the excess oil gets soaked.
- Serve it hot with Mayonnaise and Ketchup.
- Coriander leaves and finely chopped onions can be used as a supplement to the dish.( Indians specially love this part with any snack.)
