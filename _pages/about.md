👩 Welcome to my world of exploration and creativity!

I'm Manpa Barman, a passionate graduate student from the charming city of Stuttgart, Germany. I am pursuing my masters in Information Technology with a focus in deep learning and embedded systems. As I navigate through this fascinating field, I'm excited to share my experiences and knowledge with you.

### The Artistic Soul 🎨 🎶

Beyond the world of algorithms and coding, I'm an artist at heart. Music, especially Indian classical, has a special place in my soul. I am currently learning music theory and play some ukelele on the go. 

You can also catch glimpses of my creative expressions on my [instagram](https://www.instagram.com/the_untitled_space/) handle, where I frequently share my artistic endeavors.

### Blogging About Life's Experiences

In addition to my technical pursuits and artistic endeavors, I've embarked on a personal journey of self-expression through writing. I've been chronicling my life and experiences on Medium, however have been a bit inconsistent oweing to the busy schedule of a grad student. I hope to be more regular in the future.

You can check out my previous blogs [here](https://medium.com/@manpabarman).

### Health is a priority 🏃‍♀️
Being a graduate student living alone and studying abroad health is an utmost priority. I have enrolled myself in fencing classes and also go for regular walks or runs. I also love to cook and try out new healthy recipes.




Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing my world with you!
Recently I have also started writing personal blogs on my life and experiences so far on medium. You can even check that [here](https://medium.com/@manpabarman).
